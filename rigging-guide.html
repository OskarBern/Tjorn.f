Master The Method: The Skärgård Rigging Guide

The Skärgård Series is designed for a natural, yet powerful action. The tail is thin-walled to maximize vibration on minimal rod movement. Avoid overworking the lure; let the plastic's density and design do the work.

Proof in the catch: TJÖRN FINESSE lures deliver results.

1. Recommended Rigging Techniques

Technique Focus Recommended Size Setup Details
Weedless Texas Rig Heavy Cover/Structure Large (15cm) Use a wide-gap, screw-lock hook (4/0 or 5/0). The weight should be pegged 6–8 inches above the lure.
Jig Head Retrieval Open Water/Mid-Depth Small (9cm) Pair with a rounded, ball-head jig (1/8 oz to 1/4 oz). Use a steady, slow retrieve with occasional pulls.
Dropshot Finesse Suspended/Finicky Fish Small (9cm) Hook the lure through the nose (no weight). Use slight slack-line shakes for maximum tail motion.
Weighted Swim Hook Shallow Grass/Pads Large (15cm) Rig on a specialized belly-weighted swim hook (3/0 to 5/0) to keep the lure horizontal.

2. Color Specific Tips

Red Head: Maximize its high-contrast appeal in **low-light conditions** (dawn/dusk) or in **stained/murky water**. Its bold silhouette triggers aggressive strikes.

Shiny Red: Best used on **sunny days** or in **clear water**. The internal flash maximizes light reflection; fish it with a vertical presentation to mimic a fleeing baitfish.
