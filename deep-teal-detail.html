Product Overview

A dense, dark teal color with an inner iridescent blue/purple shimmer. This lure is designed to maintain visibility and silhouette in the lower water column and deep shadows, making it perfect for targeting fish holding tight to the bottom structure.

Key Features

  • • Iridescent Pigment: Provides a unique flash without being overly bright.
  • • Dark Silhouette: Excellent for deep drops or targeting wary fish.
  • • Best for: Deep jigging, vertical presentations, and fishing near dark structures.
